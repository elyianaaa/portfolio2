Metamodernity Poster

This is a poster design inspired by the song "Metamodernity." The design captures the song's mood, blending gray colors and modern typography using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. One of the lyrics is "Call me when the world looks bleak", so I highlighted that specific part by using gray tones in this design.

Apps Used and References

  • Adobe Photoshop – For image manipulation and graphic design.
  • Adobe Illustrator – For vector design and typography.
  • Song: "Metamodernity" – The inspiration behind the design.

Design Concept

The concept behind this design is to give out a sense of nostalgia and love. The typography was carefully placed to create a dynamic, modern look that complements the emotions evoked by the song.

Walking Club Poster

This is a poster design inspired by a new album called "Walk." by NCT 127. The design captures the laidback vibes of the album, blending the most used colors in the album teasers using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Tools & Apps Used

  • Adobe Photoshop – Mainly for picture layout and text arrangement.
  • Adobe Illustrator – Used extensively for text style manipulation, such as warping.
  • Pinterest – For finding texture overlays to enhance the design's feel.

Concept

The concept behind this design was to experiment and have fun with the teasers released for the member. The phrase "Walking Club" was inspired by the album’s title "Walk" and also got inspired by an album namecard inclusion.
